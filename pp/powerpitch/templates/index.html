Think, Speak, Succeed

PowerPitch is an initiative taken by the Department of Cyber Security under CYBER CLUB to improve the communication skills of students. Through this program, we aim to enhance public speaking abilities, build confidence, and develop essential presentation skills that are crucial in today's professional world.
